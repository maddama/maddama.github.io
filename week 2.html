LGBTQ

Journal 07/19/2019 Siqi Li

I have found a Chinese restaurant called XiangJu, which means get together in Chinese. It sits at the east of our campus, and the food there is really amazing, better than most of the restaurants in China.

lgbtq turesure hunting Before coming to american, the thought never came to me that there is another gender other than male and female, which turned out to be 5 or even more. These are some flyers that I get from the geneq office. Since I have a class right at the barker hall, which happens to have a genderneutral restroom there. I am just lucky, I guess. Then I went to ask the advisor at the main libriry to see if there is another genderneutral restroom there. He told me that I can find one at the moffit libriry. So I went to the moffit libriry and ask the advisor at 3rd floor, and she told me there is a large one at 4th floor. Score! lucky me. Then I ask the advisor at 4th floor for more information, he told me I can check the student union. Sadly, though there's a lot of lgbtq group, but I can't track down any of them. And I was told by one of the students there that there is no exhibition about lgbtq in July. Some I googled to find out that there's a geneq office next to the student union. Then, after a lot of asking, I find the geneq office, and get those flyers. Once hearing that I am not part of the community, the officer seems to be a little upset, after showing me send me off.

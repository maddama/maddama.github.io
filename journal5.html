A Day In San Fransisco

Journal 07/19/2019 Siqi Li

As an engineering student, one thing that we must pay a visit to is the legendary golden gate bridge.

It is one of the most beautiful bridges in the world and has a reputation for conquering an impossible engineer problem in a very genius way.

Although it is possible to build a bridge twice the length of the bridge, the spirit of being creative while building it remains.

Then we went to the Palace of fine arts, which I may refer to as a palace of civil construction.

It was built to memorize the earthquake of San Fransisco, and have been restructured several times afterward.

After this, we paid a visit to Lombard Street. It is impressive how American people can take advantage of the bad environment and make it one of the most elegant streets in the world,

I should learn the spirit from it because I am terrible at coping with stress and failure.

At the end of our trip, we went to the Chinatown, a week from home, but I am already homesick, which is my stomach haha.
